OpenCity's homepage submission log.
===================================
OpenCity, another free 3d city simulator
OpenCity is another city simulator written with OpenGL & SDL. You can build some RCI zones, electric plant and roads. Besides you can also raise or lower the terrain. OpenCity 0.0.2 stable is now available for download. It is not intended to be a clone of any famous city simulator from Max*s. If you are looking for a SimCity 4 like, forget OpenCity.
